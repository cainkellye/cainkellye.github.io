Why Choose Udolingo?

🤖

Use with the AI of your choice

You can use any large language model (LLM) to generate lessons. There is a built-in prompt generator to get started. Experiment with different engines, like ChatGPT, Gemini, ClaudeAI.

🎨

Custom theme/subject

You can specify what topic you want to practice. Travels? Shopping? Politics? A list is included in the prompt generator, but you can come up with your own.

💡

Learn what's important to you

Generate a lesson about your favorite book, TV show, game or a song lyrics. Your imagination is the limit!

🔗

Share lessons

Do you like a lesson and want share with friend? Or you hand-made one for your students? Share it with a simple url!

📚

Learn from your mistakes

Whenever you feel like it, click the 'My Mistakes' button to review them, or use the 'Copy for LLM' button to create a prompt for you AI assistant to help you understand and learn from your mistakes.

🆓

Free, open source

No ads, no tracking. Open source, you can contribute. Available on GitHub for transparency and community collaboration.
