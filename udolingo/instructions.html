📚 How to Use Udolingo

🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to Udolingo! This guide will help you get the most out of your AI-powered language learning experience.


  1. Generate a Lesson with AI
    Click "Generate LLM Prompt" to create a custom prompt for your AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.). Choose your languages, difficulty level, and topic of interest.
  2. Copy and Use the Prompt
    Copy the generated prompt and paste it into your preferred AI chat service. The AI will generate a JSON lesson configuration.
  3. Load Your Lesson
    Copy the JSON response from your AI and paste it into Udolingo using "Load Lesson" → "Paste New Lesson".
  4. Start Learning
    Begin practicing with your personalized lesson! Use the word bank to build sentences and get instant feedback.
Begin!

✨ Key Features

🤖 AI Integration

Use any LLM (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) to generate personalized lessons with our built-in prompt generator.

🎨 Custom Themes

Practice with topics that interest you: travel, business, movies, books, or any subject you choose.

📱 Bidirectional Learning

Translation directions (A→B and B→A) are randomized. When the lesson starts over, they will be swapped!

📚 Mistake Tracking

Review your mistakes anytime and generate AI prompts to help you understand and learn from them.

🔗 Easy Sharing

Share lessons with friends or students using simple URLs. Perfect for educators and study groups.

💾 Save & Organize

Lessons are automatically saved locally for easy access later. Rename them to organize better, or delete if not needed.

📝 Exercise Interface

💡 Tip: Use the word bank below the exercise to build your translation. Click words to add them to your response, and use the action buttons to navigate through exercises.

Action Buttons

  • Submit: Check your answer and get feedback
  • Clear: Clear your current response
  • Skip: Move to the next exercise without answering
  • Back: Remove the last added word
  • Google Translate: Open the current prompt in Google Translate
⚠️ Note: Exercises are scored based on exact matches. Make sure to use proper capitalization and punctuation as shown in the lesson.

🎯 Advanced Tips

Creating Better Lessons with AI

  • Be Specific: The more specific your topic, the better your AI-generated lessons will be
  • Set Context: Include context like "for business meetings" or "for casual conversation"
  • Vary Difficulty: Start with easier lessons and gradually increase difficulty
  • Cultural Context: Ask your AI to include cultural notes and context

Effective Practice Strategies

  • Regular Sessions: Practice little and often rather than long, infrequent sessions
  • Review Mistakes: Use the "My Mistakes" feature to identify patterns in your errors
  • Mix Topics: Alternate between different subjects to maintain interest
  • Both Directions: Practice translation in both directions, by cycling though the lessons again, for better retention

Sharing and Collaboration

  • Study Groups: Share lesson URLs with study partners
  • Classroom Use: Teachers can create and distribute custom lessons
  • Progress Tracking: Save lessons to track your learning journey

🔧 Troubleshooting

Common Issues

❌ Lesson Won't Load

Check that your JSON is properly formatted. Use the "Config Format" guide to verify the structure.

❌ Buttons Are Disabled

Some buttons only become active after loading a lesson. Make sure you have successfully loaded content.

❌ Sharing Link Doesn't Work

Sharing links contain the entire lesson data. Make sure the URL wasn't truncated when copying.

❌ AI Generated Invalid JSON

Sometimes AI responses need editing. Check for missing commas, brackets, or quotation marks.

💡 Still having issues? Check the browser console (F12) for error messages, or visit our GitHub repository for more help and to report bugs.
